# Dream Journal

## February 01, 2024

**Raw notes:** I was walking through a library where all the books were made of glass. When I opened one the pages shattered into butterflies that flew up through a hole in the ceiling into a violet sky.

**Scene:** A vast cathedral-like library stretches into shadow, its towering shelves lined with books bound in translucent glass that catch and scatter dim amber light. A solitary figure reaches for a glass volume; as it opens, the pages fracture silently into hundreds of luminous monarch butterflies. They spiral upward in a living column through a ragged opening in the vaulted stone ceiling, beyond which a deep violet twilight sky pulses with faint auroral streaks.

**Style:** Watercolor Journal

**Tags:** `library` `transformation` `flight` `glass` `butterflies`

## November 15, 2023

**Raw notes:** i was falling through the sky and i saw the mountains

**Scene:** The vast sky, a canvas of vibrant cerulean blue, meets the jagged peaks of distant mountains below. The sensation of falling is encapsulated in the dizzying aerial view, the earth below a staircase of vertiginous ridges, their verdant greenery interspersed with bare, rocky faces. Soft, glowing sunlight bathes the silent scene, casting long, ethereal shadows that dance across the mountain landscape. The wind, a mere suggestion, whirls wisps of frosty clouds across the tranquil expanse, their nebulous forms the only testament to velocity.

## November 16, 2023

**Raw notes:** I was wading through a purple river, I saw a waterfall and felt peaceful

**Scene:** Immersed in an otherworldly landscape, the translucent purple river, rich as twilight, flows gently across the canvas. From a distance appears a waterfall, its cascading droplets shimmering in iridescent hues, seeming more like a liquid light show than a natural formation. Bathed in the serene glow of the moon, the whole scene emanates tranquility, the calming whispers of water descending outweighing any semblance of fear or panic. The faint, silver reflections on the river's surface, mirror pieces of the starry night above, adding a luminescent beauty that pulls the gaze closer.
